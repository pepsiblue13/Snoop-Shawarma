About Us

Snoop Shawarma started on April 28, 2024, in Mabolo Valenzuela City. We are only a little over a year old, but our business is improving rapidly. We travel to different provinces for events and fiestas, allowing us to interact with various people and learn about their traditions. The name “shawarma” comes from the Turkish word “çevirme,” meaning “turning,” which describes how the meat slowly rotates while cooking.

Our Values & Mission

We want to make money and provide jobs for our staff, but our priority is to satisfy our customers. We have learned to socialize and have long-term patience with different types of customers. More than being entrepreneurs, we have become a good influence on the people around us. We aim to provide good service and prove that our shawarma is truly “delicioso” at a low price.
